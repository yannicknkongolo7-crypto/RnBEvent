Privacy & Data Security

YOUR TRUST IS OUR PRIORITY

At RNB Events, we understand how personal of a journey planning your event can be. You trust us with your story, your vision, and your information — and we take that responsibility seriously. This page explains exactly how we collect, protect, and handle all data shared with us through our website, client portal, and planning process.

1. Information We Collect

To deliver the exceptional, personalized service RNB Events is known for, we may collect the following types of information:

  • Personal identification — names, email addresses, and phone numbers provided through inquiry forms or your client portal.
  • Event details — dates, venues, guest counts, preferences, and any planning notes you share.
  • Financial information — agreed service amounts and payment arrangements as documented in your signed agreement. We do not store full credit card or payment account numbers.
  • Contract and document data — signed agreements, uploaded documents, vendor information, and moodboard content saved in your portal.
  • Technical data — IP addresses and browser/device information collected at the time of contract signing for verification and security purposes.

2. How We Use Your Information

Your information is used exclusively to plan, coordinate, and deliver your event. Specifically, we use it to:

  • Create and manage your personalized client portal and planning timeline.
  • Communicate event details, updates, and important milestones with you and, where authorized, your event planner.
  • Generate and execute your service agreement and supporting documents.
  • Coordinate with vetted vendors and partners specifically assigned to your event.
  • Send post-event follow-ups and thank-you communications.

3. Data Security

We take the security of your information seriously and employ multiple layers of protection:

  • Encrypted storage — all client data is stored on secure, encrypted AWS cloud infrastructure with access controls in place.
  • Access-code protected portal — your client portal is accessible only through unique, individually issued access codes.
  • HTTPS everywhere — all data in transit between your device and our systems is encrypted via SSL/TLS.
  • Limited access — only authorized members of the RNB Events team can access your account information, and only as needed to serve you.
  • Audit logging — all significant actions within your portal and our admin system are logged for accountability and security review.

4. Confidentiality

All information shared between you and RNB Events — personal, financial, logistical, or otherwise — is treated as strictly confidential. We will never disclose your information to any third party without your prior written consent, except as required by applicable law. Vendors and partners engaged for your event receive only the information necessary to perform their specific role.

5. No Sale of Personal Data

RNB Events will never sell, rent, trade, or share your personal information for marketing or commercial purposes. Your data exists solely to serve your event planning experience with us.

6. Data Retention

Your client records are retained after your event in an archived state for administrative and legal reference. Archived clients are not visible on our active client directory. You may request permanent deletion of your records at any time by contacting us directly, and we will fulfill such requests promptly unless retention is required by law.

7. Website Analytics

Our public website uses Google Analytics to understand visitor behavior in aggregate (pages visited, time on site, device type, etc.). This data is fully anonymized and is used only to improve the website experience. No personally identifiable information is shared with Google Analytics.

8. Your Rights

You have the right to:

  • Request access to the personal information we hold about you.
  • Request correction of any inaccurate information.
  • Request deletion of your personal data from our systems.
  • Withdraw consent for communications at any time.

To exercise any of these rights, please reach out to us using the contact information below.
