Privacy & Data Security

YOUR TRUST IS OUR PRIORITY

At RNB Events, your privacy matters to us. Everything you share — from personal details to event plans — is handled with care, kept secure, and never shared without your consent.

What We Collect

We collect only what is necessary to plan and deliver your event — including your name, contact information, event details, and any content you provide through your client portal or service agreement.

How We Use It

Your information is used exclusively to coordinate your event, communicate with you and your planning team, and fulfill the services outlined in your agreement. We do not use your data for any other purpose.

How We Protect It

All client data is stored securely on encrypted cloud infrastructure. Your portal is protected by individually issued access codes, and all data in transit is encrypted via SSL/TLS. Only authorized members of the RNB Events team can access your information.

Confidentiality

We will never sell, rent, or share your personal information with third parties for marketing or commercial purposes. Vendors engaged for your event receive only what is strictly necessary to serve you.

Questions?

If you have any questions or requests regarding your personal information, we're happy to help.
